Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Ice Cream picture

Delicoius cold Ice cream for summer that is easy to make with the whole family

You will need an ice cream machine

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 1 tbs vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Combine milk, heavy cream, sugar, vanilla and salt
  2. Cook on medium low heat and stir constantly until mixture is thickened
  3. Let mixture cool and transfer to ice cream machine
  4. Churn until the consistency of soft serve, store in fridge until firm and enjoy
