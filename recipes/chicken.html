Chef John's Nashville Chicken

Nashville Chicken picture

Fried Chicken in Nashville style that really kicks!

Always enjoy with pickles

Ingredients

  • 1 whole Chicken cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of Kosher Salt

Marinade

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • quarter cup pickle brine
  • 2 tbs Louisiana style hot sauce
  • 1 large egg

Flour

  • 2 Cups ap flour
  • 2 tsp tbl salt

Sauce

  • Quarter cup of butter
  • Quarter cup of lard
  • Half tsp Pepper
  • 2 Tbs Cayenne
  • 1 Tbs Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • half tsp Garlic Powder
  • half tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup vegtable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Salt Chicken and refridgerate overnight
  2. Combine Marinade and chick, let sit covered in the fridge for 2 to 4 hours
  3. Mix flour and salt together and bread chicken
  4. Fry Chicken till internal temperature of 160 degrees F
  5. Make sauce and dip in the cooked Fried Chicken
